User Flow Description

On the first JobDocs launch, User has 2 options to continue working with the application:

  1. Log in using an existing confirmed account.
  2. Sign up - create a new account.

Log in

If User has an existing JobDocs profile, it can be used for logging in the application:

  • On the above screen, tap LOG IN.
  • Type in e-mail and password you specified when creating a JobDocs account.
  • Tap SIGN IN.

If you forgot your passowrd, just tap the Forgot password? link - then follow the steps to recover your password.

Log in with Google or Facebook

You have an alternative way to log in to JobDocs - using a Facebook or Google account. For this:

  • Tap the SIGN UP button, if you are at the initial step, or the Create new account link - if at the LOG IN screen already.
  • Tap a button with the name of the chosen social network.
  • On the appeared screen, provide your credentials for the social profile. LOG IN.

Sign up

To create a new account:

  • You can use a social network account to create a JobDocs profile. See Log in with Google or Facebook. Or follow the steps below.
  • Tap the SIGN UP button.
  • On the Create new account screen:

    you can add a photo of yourself. For this, tap the avatar - in the appeared shortcut menu select whether to take a photo using the camera on your phone or to choose from library. Or just skip this step.
  • Provide your Full Name, Email Address, Zip Code, Password (6 or more characters).
  • Tap CREATE NEW ACCOUNT

A new page appears - "What type of job do you do?".

Your account is not yet created. A few more steps required:

  • Now you need Select Job from a predefined list of jobs. The list will appear after tapping on the Select Job field.
  • Next Select Role(s) like in the previous step.
  • Tap NEXT

The final step is selecting categories of documents you want to store in JobDocs:

  • Select categories of documents from the list. Some of them are already preselected based on your Job. Tap NEXT.
  • Here is all the gathered information you have provided above. If anything needs corrections, tap Edit next to the needed information block.

  • CONFIRM
  • GO TO HOME

When finished creating an account, you get to the Home Screen of the JobDocs application. See the Home Screen article to learn about how to use it.
