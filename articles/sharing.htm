User Flow Description

JobDocs allows sharing documents and categories. You have 3 options to share:

  • a single document

  • a bundle of documents from different categories

  • a category with all included documents

    • .

Bundle export

Using the Export button

On the Home Screen, tap the button.

  • Select categories you want to share files from.

  • To expand categories, in order to see included files, tap the needed category name.

  • Select documents you want to export.

  • SHARE

  • Select the sharing resource from the list of available on your device - share like you always do with normal files

Sharing a category

Using the category shortcut menu

To share a single specific category, call the category shortcut menu.

  • Tap Share

  • Select the sharing resource from the list of available on your device - share like you always do with normal files

Sharing a document

Using the document shortcut menu

To share a single specific document, call the document shortcut menu.

  • Tap Share

  • Select the sharing resource from the list of available on your device - share like you always do with normal files
